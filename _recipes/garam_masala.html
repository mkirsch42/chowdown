--- layout: recipe title: Garam Masala tags: [indian] recipe_yield: 4 Tbsp ingredients: - 1 Tbsp cumin seeds - 1 Tbsp coriander seeds - 4 black cardamom pods - 1/2 cinnamon stick - 1/2 tsp black peppercorns - 1/2 tsp mustard seeds directions: - Add spices to a stainless steel pan and toast over medium-high heat until just barely smoking. - Grind the spices into a powder. references: - name: Adam Ragusea url: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GcWYXQ5vILs ---
