--- layout: recipe title: Lemonade image: lemonade.jpg tags: [beverage] recipe_yield: 2 quarts ingredients: - 1 cup lemon juice, fresh - Zest of 1 lemon - 3/4 cup white sugar - 1/4 cup honey - Salt - Water directions: - Heat the sugar, honey, and 1 cup water over medium-high heat, whisking until dissolved and steaming. - Remove from the heat and add the lemon zest, lemon juice, and a pinch of salt. Stir to combine. - Steep the mixture for at least half an hour. - Strain into a pitcher and mix in 6-7 cups of cold water. Refrigerate until cool. ---
