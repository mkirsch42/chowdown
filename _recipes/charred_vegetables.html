--- layout: recipe title: Charred Vegetables image: charred_vegetables.jpg tags: [side, vegetables, vegetarian] ingredients: - 1 green bell pepper, sliced into 1/4" rings - 1 yellow onion, sliced into 1/4" rings - Kosher salt - Neutral oil directions: - Heat neutral oil in a cast iron pan over high heat until smoking. - Add the vegetables to the pan and sprinkle lightly with salt. Do not move the vegetables for about a minute. - Once the vegetables are charred on one side, flip the and char the other side. - Remove from the heat before the vegetables are fully cooked. references: - name: Adam Ragusea url: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GcWYXQ5vILs ---
