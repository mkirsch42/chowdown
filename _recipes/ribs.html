--- layout: recipe title: Ribs image: ribs.jpg tags: [meat, pork, dinner, grill] recipe_yield: 2 Chops components: - bbq_sauce - pork_rub ingredients: - 2 Racks Pork Ribs directions: - Preheat the oven (or grill with indirect heat) to 350°F. - Remove the membrane from the ribs if necessary. - Generously apply the rub to the ribs on all sides. - Tightly wrap the ribs in aluminum foil, sealing completely to prevent leaks. - Cook the ribs for 2:30 - 3:00, flipping halfway through. The ribs should be very tender. - Unwrap the ribs and allow them to cool. Meanwhile, make the barbecue sauce. Optionally, add about 1/4 cup of the meat juices to the sauce while simmering. - Heat a grill on high heat. - Baste one side of the ribs with the barbecue sauce. Place on the grill sauce-side down, then baste the other side. - Cook the ribs until the sauce becomes sticky and grill marks have formed, then flip. - Repeat basting and flipping up to a total of 4 times. - Remove the ribs from the grill and allow to cool. references: - name: Joshua Weissman url: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=RZE67tUcT7I ---
