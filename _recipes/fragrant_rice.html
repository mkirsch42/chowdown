--- layout: recipe title: Fragrant Rice image: rice.jpg imagecredit: https://cooking.nytimes.com/recipes/1019204-basic-stovetop-rice tags: [side] recipe_yield: 4-6 cups ingredients: - 2 cups Jasmine rice - ~3 cups water - Salt - Black pepper - 2-3 star anise - 3-4 white cardamom pods, removed from their shells directions: - Very thoroughly rinse the rice with a strainer and/or bowl. - Add the rice to a stockpot along with slightly less than 1.5x water by volume (some water was already absorbed during rinsing). - Season to taste with salt, pepper, star anise, and cardamom. - Cover the pot with a lid and bring to a boil over high heat. Do not remove the lid; listening for the boil may be required. - Immediately reduce to medium-low heat and simmer for 10 minutes, keeping the lid on. - Remove from the heat and allow the rice to steam with the lid on for 5 minutes. - Remove the lid. There should be ~1/4 inch steam holes throughout the rice. Fluff the rice with a fork. ---
