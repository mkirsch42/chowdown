--- layout: recipe title: Brownies image: brownies.jpg tags: [baking, dessert] recipe_yield: 9 brownies ingredients: - 1 stick butter - 1/2 cup cocoa powder - 14oz sweetened condensed milk - 1 cup powdered sugar - Kosher salt - 2 teaspoons vanilla - 2 teaspoons molasses (optional) - 1 teaspoon instant espresso powder - 1 egg yolk - 90g all-purpose flour - 2 oz baking chocolate, 60-75% cocoa directions: - Preheat the oven to 325°F. - Lightly brown the butter in a medium pot, stirring constantly to prevent the milk solids from sticking to the bottom. - Add the cocoa powder to the butter and cook for about 1 minute. - Remove from the heat and add the sweetened condensed milk and combine. - Add the sugar and stir until no more clumps of sugar remain. Some milk solids will be in the batter; this is fine. - Mix in a pinch of salt, the vanilla, molasses, espresso powder, and egg yolk. Mix in the flour with a wooden spoon. - Chop the chocolate into small pieces and mix in after the batter cools off a bit. - Spray a 8x8" or 9x9" baking pan with oil and line with parchment paper. Pour the batter into the pan; do not try to spread or level the batter. - Bake for 30-40 minutes or until a skewer comes out mostly clean. Let cool at least 30 minutes before cutting. references: - name: Adam Ragusea url: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lhnzdRD-hX8 ---
